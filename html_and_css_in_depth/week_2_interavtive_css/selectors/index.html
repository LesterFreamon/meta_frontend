About Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a cozy restaurant located in the heart of Chicago. We specialize in serving delicious and healthy dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Our menu features a variety of options, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free choices. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a romantic dinner, we have something for everyone.

Our Menu

Explore our mouthwatering menu that includes a wide range of appetizers, main courses, desserts, and beverages. Each dish is carefully crafted by our talented chefs to ensure a delightful dining experience.
